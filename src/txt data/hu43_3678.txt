 More Russia sanctions to provoke dangerous situation in Eu
 Tougher sanctions against Russia could destabilize the country and provoke an “even more dangerous” situation in Europe and have negative consequences for the entire world, German Vice-Chancellor Economic Affairs and Energy Minister has warned. "Those who want it, provoke an even more dangerous situation for all of us in Europe," Sigmar Gabriel said in an interview with the Bild am Sonntag newspaper on Sunday. "Those who are seeking to even more destabilize Russia from the economic and political point of view are pursuing quite different goals."
