 New User Registrations Information
 If you are a new user who just signed up and you only see the "Support and Suggestions" forum, you have currently NOT verified your account via e-mail that was sent to you through the registration process. If you did not receive an e-mail, please send a PM to Shian Kiri Once you have been verified, you are now in a Drifter usergroup. In order to post in other forums, please introduce yourself in the Introductions forum and after a couple of hours, the forum will re-verify your account and give you access to other forums. If after 24 hours, you are still unable to post elsewhere, simply log out and log back in. If you are still unable to post anywhere else, send a PM to Shian Kiri
