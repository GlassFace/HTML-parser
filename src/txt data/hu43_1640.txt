[Fantasy] Kyoukai no Kanata (Beyond the Boundary)
 Kyoukai no Kanata Beyond the Boundary (境界の彼方 Kyōkai no Kanata) is a Japanese light novel series by Nagomu Torii, with illustrations provided by Chise Kamoi. The work won an honorable mention in the Kyoto Animation Award competition in 2011. Kyoto Animation has published three volumes since June 2012. An anime adaptation by Kyoto Animation began airing on October 2, 2013.
