    What song currently fits your mood?

    We all have days where we listen to a genre specific sound, simply because our mood is in it, or isn't. Similar to the "currently listening to.." thread, but with more depth, and is to be a complete reflection of your mood. I think it'd be a little interesting to get into the heads of the people at A4; see what makes them tick. So, it can be a slow, sappy song today, and then be an upbeat rap ballad the next day. But to keep it discussion worthy; why does the song you picked at that given time, explain your mood? It doesn't have to be philosophical. Maybe you've had too much sugar and you're on a sugar high and you're listening to some anime soundtrack, or whatever. So then, what reflects your mood at the moment?

    Song that reflects my mood at the moment:     Where Do We Go from Here?

    Artist: Filter.

    Why?     It's in my playlist, and I used to listen to it heavily a number of years back. I'm in one of those indifferent moods, where everything is appearing in different shades of grey. (Dear god, no 50 Shades of sh!t jokes, please).
