Generally, I am the typical Asian student who focuses on her studies, avoids detention, complete my assignments (and on time) and tries to excel, but I'm also pretty cold-hearted and like to pull strings in the background, so that people do not get in my way. Ever.

I was also someone looking for trouble - not with teachers, but with the boys /facetable. That didn't end too well. Otherwise, I was your typical stereotype of an Asian and basically fell asleep in every class and ra ped everyone in exams.

    In 2012, I had changed into the better class of my high school and had made many trustworthy and amazing friends, those which were true and weren't temporary or fake. During my second year of high school, I wasn't as naive or immature as I was in year 7 and actually started working a little harder and unexpectedly still ranked the same as I did, the previous year. I was also that one girl who constantly fell in love (secretly) between two of my guy friends.

    Sooo yeah. This year, I'm just a pretty bland and boring student of 2013's Year 9 students...Still studying (starting to struggle though) and I'm practically in a class where I am the foreigner - literally.
