    Just sat through 1-7, and i do think the show is quite good. It portrays the emotional turmoil of despair very well down to each individual. I really enjoy the show starting essentially pre-conflict and watching the characters change during their indoctrination into the country's military. I liked how there is a subtle love interest, and how its not over the top, it isnt even completely clear as well. One of my favorite elements is the show's ability to emulate the harshest of realities when it comes to combat, which gave it a very, "Blue Gender" vibe. Come to think of it most of the reasons why i thought Blue Gender was done so well reoccur in this anime.

    And i was very relieved i didnt see huge genitals, everyone was thinking the same thing, I'm just saying it.

    The only problems i have with the show as of yet are due to lack of information which of course comes with time, and is probably part of the reason i watched all 7 episodes back to back, which is a good thing. Its just of course the mysteries are very well done and I yearn to have some light shed on them.
