    Real books:

    You can feel them, flip the pages and run your hands along the ink.
    You can smell them, the old and new ones, the sweet aroma of paper.
    You can use them to decorate your home, coffee table books, magazines, anything.
    You can study with them, highlighters, post it notes and pens written in textbooks.
    You can shop for them, go out and drink your Starbucks while browsing through the endless shelves.
    You can read them anywhere and not worry about severe damage that would prevent you from reading them.

    Ebooks:

    You can download them.
    You can read them.
    You can edit font size and various details on the page.
    You can never read them just anywhere, they might get wet and be destroyed.
    Humankind cannot gain anything without first sacrificing something else in return. To obtain, something of equal value must be lost.
