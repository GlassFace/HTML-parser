    Sakura Trick



    Synopsis: Haruka Takayama and Yū Sonoda were inseparable during their junior high school years, but upon entering their senior high school, they end up being seated on opposite sides of the classroom. Having to spend time with more friends, the two decide to do something to make their relationship special.

    This is a bit of a surprise to see coming to anime, as this manga is supposedly a full-blown Yuri romance.
    BL and Trap were the buzzwords of the last couple seasons, so I guess it's about time, lol. xD Not that I minded the BL and the traps, mind you... they made for some very amusing and humorous anecdotes, but a Yuri romance will be refreshing too!

    This is set to air in the winter of 2014, so I'm guessing the January season.
