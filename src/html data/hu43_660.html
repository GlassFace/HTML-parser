Valvrave the Liberator Season 2

The second season of laser space vampire high schoolers piloting suicidal mechs against space-nazis and space-USA.

Kill la Kill

Ryuko is searching for her father's murderer, her only clue half of a pair of giant scissors. She transfers into Honnoji Academy, a dystopian high school ruled by the student council who possess Goku Uniforms; uniforms imbued with super powers.

Pokemon Origin

A reboot/retelling of the original Pokemon game, featuring Red and Green as the protagonist and rival. Much grittier and truer to the Pokemon manga Adventures in tone and direction.

Kyoukai no Kanata

A pair of high schoolers, a half youmu boy and a girl who can wield blood magic, wind up involved in super natural events occurring in their town.

Hajime no Ippo Rising

Long awaited new season of the well-known anime and manga, about a young boxer named Ippo rising up through Japan's ranks, as he aims for a match against his rival, Miyata.

Space Dandy

The Dandiest Man in Space, Dandy, is an alien hunter searching for undiscovered species with his robot assistant QT and a cat alien named Meow.

Samurai Flamenco

An eccentric male model decides to become a super hero in order to become a hero of justice, despite having no super powers.

    This new series predates Stand Alone Complex by 3 years so technically it's a prequel. Part of the plot has to do with Matoko meeting Batou for the first time and him thinking that she is a criminal.

    Plus she just seems to be acting like a younger, much less confident version of Matoko in that trailer. I get the distinct impression that she's less experienced but still fully capable of messing people up.

    I've been holding off on commenting on all the changes, especially with the character designs and everything. I don't think I hate them as much as everyone else, the silhouettes are good and distinctive and I can see the "younger" part of their designs. The music is pretty jarring, though. But it also kind of fits with the more youthful feel of the series as a whole.

    I prefer the English cast personally but nothing about the Japanese cast strikes me as flat-out wrong or anything. Interestingly, Maaya Sakamoto, the lady taking over Matoko, actually plays the younger Matoko in the original SAC series. Seems to fit pretty damn well to me.

    As usual the animation looks goddamn amazing.

    I'm pretty excited for this.
