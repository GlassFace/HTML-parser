Hajime no Ippo Rising

Space Dandy

    Have to agree that if I want to see shitty image macros I'll go to le reddit.

    I got +0 LP in gold 1 for three wins in a row. I then lost one and went down by 6 points.

    The leagues system is very poorly done and the fact that there hasn't been more fan outrage over this just shows the profound level of dicksucking the average fan is willing to do to appease Phreak and other Riot workers. The system was introduced under the logic that in an ELO system a number like 1850 or 2000 or 1138 means nothing to the player because he/she doesn't understand where that places him/her in the rankings, so Riot introduced a leagues system where you're put into a group with a certain number of people and you climb up from there. This was supposed to make your progress transparent where at certain breakpoints you'd feel a sense of accomplishment, or even single wins would place you closer to your goal.

    Unfortunately in their application of the system they decided to tack on an unwanted hidden MMR system to restrict your progress if this number is not on par with your league ranking. I don't understand why they did this because they essentially took the old ELO system, hid it, put a bunch of banners and borders on new leagues, and then forced you to abide by the old rules anyway. At least previously when a player was 1633 he realized he was actually 1633 and a ways away from platinum. Now a player can be silver 1 or gold 1 and either be incredibly close to the next league or a very long way away. Using tools like LoLNexus or some other hidden MMR checkers you can kind of guesstimate where you actually stand but it makes my head hurt wondering why this should be necessary.

    Hopefully in season 4 this system dies. I haven't really had interest in playing League in a while so it doesn't matter much to me I guess, but with Riot consistently making a lot of really cool and well thought out changes I can't figure out why they've been so stubborn with their stance on hidden MMR.
