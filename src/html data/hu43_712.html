    [Fairy Tail] Does it get better?

    I've been reading alot of good things about Fairy Tail lately. It gets compared with the other long shounen alot, like Naruto, One Piece, and Bleach. I notice alot of people favor it out the bunch though.

    I watched around 20 episodes I think when it first came out, then I dropped it. The 2 main reasons was, 1: at the end of each episode the main character used the same one move to defeat the enemy and 2: a few of the episodes felt like filler.

    Now I did like alot of the characters and the main premise of the story. I felt like it did have some potential. So I'm wondering does the combat get more advanced and how bad are the fillers now that the series has been out awhile?

    Since Fairy Tail is not an anime with one major plot, the plot changed along with the storyline, one after another, so maybe some of the episodes did feels like out-of-nowhere episodes. The combats are roughly the same, old moves being reused, but as always, some new moves are added into the fray, mostly finishing moves though. The latest story arc offer more variety of moves in combats since it involves a lot of new character and new strength after the time skip.

    I myself enjoy this anime a lot, and I don't see why you shouldn't re-watch this anime.
