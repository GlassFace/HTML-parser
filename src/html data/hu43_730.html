    Date A live


    Thirty years ago a strange phenomena called a "spacequake" devastated the center of Eurasia, claiming the lives of at least 150 million people. Since then, smaller spacequakes plague the world on a irregular basis. Shidō Itsuka, a seemingly ordinary high schooler comes across a mysterious girl at the ground zero of a spacequake and learns from his sister Kotori she is one of the "Spirits" who are the real cause of the spacequakes that occur when they manifest themselves in the world. He also learns that Kotori is the captain of the airship Ratatoskr and recruits him to make use of his mysterious ability to seal the Spirits' powers and stop them from being a threat to mankind. However, there is a catch: to seal a Spirit, he must make her fall in love with him.
    Summary by Wikipedia

     Spoiler
    The team where Shidou belongs choose a more safer way to handle the spirits, which is by dating them. On the other hand, another team called AST (Anti-Spirit Team) utilizes the more brutal way to handle the spirits, which is by destroying them. So there are two different forces that handled the spirits. The fights will be mostly between the AST and the spirits, and not with the team where Shidou is in. So the one that do the dating thing and the one that do the fighting thing is not the same person.

    If you put that into account, then the story will make sense.
