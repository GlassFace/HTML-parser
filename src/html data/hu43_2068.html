    Hitsugi no Chaika (Chaika the Coffin Princess)




    English Title: Chaika the Coffin Princess

    Toru Acura is a 20-year-old retired soldier meandering through life now that the war has ended. He encounters Chaika Trabant, a 14-year-old sorceress carrying a coffin, and follows her in hopes of finding meaning to his life again. The two travel with Toru's adopted sister, Akari, the employed member of the group and thus Toru's source of income.

    ...

    Hitsugi no Chaika is a Japanese light novel series written by Ichirō Sakaki and illustrated by Namaniku ATK. As of January 2014, eight volumes have been published by Fujimi Shobo under their Fujimi Fantasia Bunko imprint. It has been adapted into two manga series. An anime adaptation was announced on July 2013.

    Coming for Spring season of 2014, so look for it around April or so!
